Our Vision

Our vision is to create a sustainable and compassionate world through volunteerism. We believe that everyone has the potential to make a difference in the world, and we are committed to providing the tools and resources to make that difference.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower youths to volunteer and create positive social change. We strive to develop a sense of service and compassion in our students, and to equip them with the skills and knowledge to be effective volunteers.

About us

SOVIC is a non-profit organization based in Assam Engineering College, Guwahati. Our mission is to inspire and empower youths to volunteer and create transformative social change. We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, and in the importance of diversity and inclusion. We strive to create an environment of support, understanding, and mutual respect. Join us in our mission to make a sustainable difference in society.

We invite you to join us on our mission to create a more compassionate and sustainable world.
